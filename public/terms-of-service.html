Consent Requirements

You are solely responsible for obtaining all legally required consents from all parties to any recorded communication BEFORE the call is recorded. Consent requirements vary significantly by jurisdiction:

  • Two-Party Consent Jurisdictions: These states (California, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, Illinois, Maryland, Michigan, Montana, Nevada, New Hampshire, Pennsylvania, Washington) require explicit consent from EVERY party to a call before recording.   • One-Party Consent Jurisdictions: Other states allow recording if at least one party (you) consents, even if others do not consent. However, some states have exceptions.
  • International Considerations: Recording laws vary by country and may impose strict restrictions or prohibitions.

  • You MUST obtain explicit verbal or written consent from all parties in two-party consent states
